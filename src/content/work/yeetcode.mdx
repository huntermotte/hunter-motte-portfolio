Yeetcode is a coding-practice platform built to help developers actually get
better — not just grind problems, but work through them step by step and watch
their progress add up over time. It runs on iOS, Android, and the web.

## Key features

- Step-by-step problem solving that builds real skills
- Progress tracking as you practice
- Curated problems across every experience level
- Available on iOS, Android, and the web
