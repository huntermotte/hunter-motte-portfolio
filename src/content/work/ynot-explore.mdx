yNot helps you decide where to go. When you and your partner can't pick a place
to eat, or your crew can't agree on a bar for the game, yNot surfaces the right
spot using aggregated reviews from Google and Yelp, with robust filtering for
restaurants, bars, concerts, museums, and more.

## Key features

- Aggregated reviews from Google and Yelp
- Filter by rating, distance, and location
- Discover restaurants, bars, concerts, museums, and more
- Available on iOS and Android
