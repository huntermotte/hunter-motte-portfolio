The Ledger keeps friendly bets honest. Propose a wager on your next trip or
game day, record outcomes as they happen, and let The Ledger work out who owes
whom — then settle up in a tap with Venmo deep-links.

## Key features

- Track peer-to-peer bets among friends
- Propose wagers and record outcomes as you go
- Automatic settlement of who owes whom
- Pay up instantly with Venmo deep-links
- Secure Sign in with Apple and Google
